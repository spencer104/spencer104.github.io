Impact+ Business Plan

Hello! 

               For my Fall 2021 semester, I was part of a 10-person team that developed
    a business plan to bring a product to market. The plan focused on four key
    topics for a business: marketing, operations, risk, and finances. My role on the 
    team was to lead the finance and operations teams by creating and allocating 
    tasks, as well as compiling everyone's work. My favorite part of this project was 
    being able to utilize and learn new skills not directly related to the project.
    I had fun designing the product, learning how to use Photoshop, and reaching out 
    to friends to turn the 3D print model into a laser-cut acrylic model. Overall, 
    I enjoyed this project because it forced me to think and act dynamically 
    by integrating multiple disciplines into one singular project.
