Dashbaord Project

For this project, I created an interactive dashboard within Google Sheets that allows a small business owner to track their expenses and the performance of their business. To use this dashboard, the user fills in their daily indicators. Then, the sheet translates the daily indicators into a weekly version of the data. This process enables me to utilize the weekly data to develop graphs and a simple linear regression model to estimate key statistics. Additionally, by converting the data into a weekly format, it reduces variance within the data.
